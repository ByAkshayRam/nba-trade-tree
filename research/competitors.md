# Competitor Research: Fanspo & Spotrac

## Fanspo.com

**What it is:** Social network for sports fans — trade machine, mock drafts, discussions

**UI/UX Strengths:**
- Trade machine is interactive and visual
- User-generated content (mock trades, discussions)
- Social features (likes, comments, shares)
- Mobile-responsive design

**UI/UX Weaknesses:**
- Cluttered interface with lots of ads
- Information density is high — can feel overwhelming
- Navigation isn't always intuitive
- Dated visual design (feels 2018-era)

**What to take:**
- Interactive trade builder concept
- User engagement mechanics (but simplify)
- Trade validation logic (salary matching)

**What to improve:**
- Cleaner, more modern aesthetic
- Focus on visualization over data tables
- Better information hierarchy

---

## Spotrac.com

**What it is:** Authoritative source for sports contracts, salaries, cap data

**UI/UX Strengths:**
- Comprehensive data — the gold standard for contract info
- Clean data tables with sorting/filtering
- Good search functionality
- News integration with financial context
- Team pages with full roster breakdowns

**UI/UX Weaknesses:**
- Very data-heavy — intimidating for casual fans
- Tables everywhere — not visual enough
- Limited interactivity beyond filtering
- Paywall for advanced features
- Mobile experience could be better

**What to take:**
- Data depth and accuracy standards
- Team roster layout structure
- Player profile pages with contract history
- News/updates tied to transactions

**What to improve:**
- Transform tables into visual stories
- Make trade lineage the hero, not just contracts
- Modern dark theme (like Mission Control)
- Interactive tree visualization instead of static tables

---

## Design Principles for NBA Trade Tree

Based on competitor analysis, our approach:

1. **Visual-first:** Trees and graphs > tables
2. **Clean aesthetic:** Modern dark theme, minimal clutter
3. **Focused scope:** Trade lineage only (not contracts, salaries)
4. **Interactive exploration:** Click to expand, hover for details
5. **Shareable:** Easy to screenshot/share on social
6. **Mobile-friendly:** Works on phones for casual browsing
7. **Fast:** Instant search, snappy transitions
